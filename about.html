I’m Nicole, your friendly Twin Cities-area photographer specializing in 35mm film, mini polaroids, and filmic-graded digital video. I got my start in film photography on a Pikachu camera in the early 2000s - fast forward to today, and I’m still all about capturing candid moments, whether it be during an event, or an unplanned break in posing during portrait shoots. Ultimately it’s seeing others’ happy moments come to life with the colors of film that makes my heart sing and my retinas dance.

Other things to know about me? For starters, I probably wouldn't even be doing this today if it weren't for my bff of 7+ years (my husband), Cristian. I'm super blessed to be his wife (read: I married up)! And a fun tidbit? Any aerial drone footage you see around here is his - check out the Video page to learn more!

When we're not shooting or snapping away - I wanted to say you could find us on a hike or at an airport jetting off, but let's be honest...we're mostly cleaning and doing house projects these days lol.

Now that I've shared a little bit about me, I wanna get to know YOU! Let's chat!
