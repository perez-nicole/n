FAQS

Do you do weddings? If so, is there a different pricing system for them?

Absolutely I do weddings! And I don't have a "special" pricing system for weddings; the above pricing applies for all my events & shoots, weddings included. I put my whole heart into every event I do, and I don't believe in charging more simply because it's your "big day." So let's get you hitched!


Would you shoot both photo and video for my event?


Generally, I only do photo or video for a given event, since doing both would inevitably mean missing big moments on one medium or the other. However, I'm always happy to recommend other photographers/videographers for the medium I'm not doing for you!


How soon after my shoot/event will I get the photos/video back?


For 2-hour photoshoots, you'll get your photos back within 2-3 weeks, and for larger events (4+ hours), you'll usually get your photos back within 4-6 weeks. Every event and shoot is unique, though, so a firm delivery timeline will be agreed on during our consultation call and included in our contract.
